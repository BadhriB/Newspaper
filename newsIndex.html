N440K in Gujarat: The new super infective virus strain on the block

AHMEDABAD: An especially infective Covid variant known as N440K has a mutation in its spike protein and had lingered for quite a while in the southern states along with the now dominant double mutant B.1.617. It is B.1.617 that has wreaked havoc across the country during the second wave. But few know that the N440K variant was also seen in Gujarat in at least nine Covid samples of the 293 tested between January and April 24 this year. This fact was revealed in a research paper published recently by the Academy for Scientific and Innovative Research (AcSIR), headquartered on the CSIR campus in Ghaziabad.

Terrorist hideout busted in J&K’s Rajouri; arms, ammunition seized

Security forces raided a terrorist hideout in the Manyal area of Jammu and Kashmir's Rajouri district on Friday and seized a huge cache of arms and ammunition. Based on information of suspicious movement in the upper reaches of Thanamandi's Azmatabad area, the local Rashtriya Rifles Battalion, Jammu and Kashmir Police and Special Operations Group (SOG) launched a joint combing and search operation in Manyal, Danna and Kopra.

An FIR was registered in this connection at the Thanamandi police station and an investigation initiated, they said.

Fauci email leak gives insight into early days of Covid response, raises critical questions

NEW DELHI: The US government’s top infectious disease scientist, Dr Anthony Fauci, is in the eye of a storm after hundreds of his private emails were recently made public by two US media outlets. The emails have revealed details of his correspondence between February and April 2020 when Covid-19 cases were surging throughout the US. Buzzfeed News and The Washington Post gained access to the thousands of pages of communication under the Freedom of Information Act. While the email trove gives information about how Fauci was juggling crisis calls, Republicans have pointed out that the emails verify their claims about the top official misleading the US public on Covid-19 origins.
